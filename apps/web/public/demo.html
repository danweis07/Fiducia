Consumer Banking

Everything your members expect from a modern digital banking experience.

💳

Accounts & Transactions

Checking, savings, CDs, money market. Full transaction history with search, filters, and enriched merchant data.

💸

Transfers & P2P

Internal, external, wire, and P2P transfers. Scheduled, recurring, and standing instructions.

📝

Bill Pay

Biller directory search, payee enrollment, one-time and recurring payments, e-bills, rush delivery.

📱

Remote Deposit (RDC)

Camera-based check deposit with image quality validation, OCR, and status tracking.

💳

Card Controls

Lock/unlock, spending limits, travel notices, replacement requests, contactless management.

📊

Financial Management

Spending categories, monthly trends, budgets, net worth, recurring transaction detection.

🎯

Savings Goals

Create goals, track progress, automated contributions. Visual progress indicators.

💬

Secure Messaging

Encrypted member-to-staff messaging with attachments, threading, and read receipts.

⚠️

Disputes (Reg E)

File transaction disputes, track status, provisional credits, document upload.

📄

Statements & Documents

E-statements, document vault, tax forms. Download as PDF.

👥

Joint Accounts

Multi-owner management, invite co-owners, per-owner permissions.

🛡️

Overdraft Protection

Configure backup accounts, opt-in/out controls, protection preferences.

AI Platform

Not a bolted-on chatbot. AI that understands banking and takes action.

Transfer $200 from checking to savings
I'll transfer $200 from your Checking (****4521) to Savings (****7832). Your new checking balance will be $3,142.50. Confirm?
Yes, and alert me if checking goes below $500
Done! Transfer complete. I've also created a spending alert: you'll get a push notification if your checking balance drops below $500. Anything else?
🧠

Intent Classification

Natural language → action. "Lock my card" executes the card lock, not a FAQ response.

💡

Proactive Insights

Overdraft prediction, spending anomalies, subscription creep, rate opportunities.

⚙️

Automation Rules

Members create rules in plain English: "Round up every purchase and save the difference."

📚

RAG Knowledge Base

Per-tenant document ingestion. AI answers with your rates, your policies, your hours.
